# Research

> Industry insights, studies, data, and analysis you reference and build upon.

---

## What Goes Here

Research is your **knowledge base of insights** - the studies, data, trends, and analysis that inform your thinking and support your arguments. This is your evidence library.

### Include
- ✅ Research papers and studies you cite
- ✅ Industry benchmarks and statistics
- ✅ Competitive analysis and observations
- ✅ Trend analysis and predictions
- ✅ Your own original research
- ✅ Expert insights and interviews
- ✅ Case studies and examples

### Don't Include
- ❌ Your own frameworks (those go in `/frameworks/`)
- ❌ Generic common knowledge
- ❌ Unverified claims or opinions without source

---

## Research Types

### Industry Studies
**Purpose**: External research that validates your approaches
**Example**: "LinkedIn Algorithm Study 2025"

### Benchmark Data
**Purpose**: Data points to reference in client work
**Example**: "Content Marketing Benchmarks by Industry"

### Trend Analysis
**Purpose**: Your analysis of emerging patterns
**Example**: "Authenticity Trends in B2B Content"

### Competitive Research
**Purpose**: What others are doing and saying
**Example**: "Top Content Strategists - Positioning Analysis"

### Original Research
**Purpose**: Studies or analysis you've conducted
**Example**: "Survey: What B2B Buyers Actually Want in Content"
