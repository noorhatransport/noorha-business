# Tools & Resources

> Your curated stack of tools, software, resources, and recommendations.

---

## What Goes Here

Tools is your **resource library** - the specific tools, platforms, resources, and materials you use, trust, and recommend. This helps AI give accurate recommendations that align with your actual preferences.

### Include
- ✅ Software and platforms you use/recommend
- ✅ Books, courses, and learning resources
- ✅ Templates and toolkits
- ✅ Agencies or service providers you trust
- ✅ Assessment tools and frameworks
- ✅ Communities and networks

### Don't Include
- ❌ Generic "best tools" lists without your evaluation
- ❌ Tools you've never used or can't vouch for
- ❌ Outdated resources you no longer recommend
