## Instructions for Using This Template

This template is for capturing "lost thoughts" - those valuable ideas that emerge in conversations, reading, or random moments of insight.

### When to Create an Idea File:
- You have an insight you don't want to lose
- You notice a pattern worth documenting
- You think "this could become content"
- You want to remember a valuable observation

### How to Use:
1. Duplicate this template
2. Rename with descriptive title
3. Fill in as much as you can immediately
4. Don't overthink - capture and move on
5. Review periodically to develop ideas further

### What Happens Next:
- Ideas can be developed into Substack posts
- Multiple ideas can be combined into larger pieces
- Ideas inform your positioning and messaging
- The `/capture` command will automate this process
