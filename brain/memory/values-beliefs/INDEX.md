## How These Files Work Together

```
Core Values (What you believe)
    ↓
Principles (How you operate)
    ↓
POVs (What you think differently)
    ↓
Frameworks (How you think and work)
```

### The Hierarchy

1. **Core Values** are your foundation - immutable beliefs that define who you are
2. **Principles** are how you apply those values in professional practice
3. **POVs** are where your values lead to different perspectives on [Field] and business
4. **Frameworks** are the mental models and methodologies you've developed from experience
