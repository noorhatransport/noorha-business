## Situation Brief

Air traffic between Saudi Arabia and Kuwait is suspended due to the Iran conflict (US/Israel strikes on Iran, late Feb 2026). Saudia and Flynas extended cancellations repeatedly. Airspace closures in Iran, Iraq, Bahrain, Kuwait, Syria, Israel are ongoing. Kuwait Airways ran repatriation flights for Kuwaiti nationals via land. This makes cross-border road transfer the ONLY reliable option — and demand is peaking NOW.
