# Project: Bahrain ↔ Dammam Airport / Riyadh Airport — Landing Pages & Google Ads Campaign
**Status**: In Progress
**Started**: March 12, 2026
**Priority**: URGENT — Bahrain flights closed, travelers must use Saudi airports
**Embassy Referrals**: Italian Embassy, Spanish Embassy (active recommendations)

---

## Situation Brief

Flights from/to Bahrain are closed due to the Iran conflict (same airspace closures affecting Saudi-Kuwait routes). Travelers needing to fly internationally must now travel overland to **Dammam Airport (DMM)** or **Riyadh Airport (RUH)** to catch flights. This creates massive demand for cross-border taxi services from Bahrain to Saudi airports.

**Key differentiator**: Noorha Transport has been recommended by the **Italian Embassy** and **Spanish Embassy** in Bahrain. High volume of enquiries through embassy referrals — this is a MAJOR trust signal.

**Competitive advantage**: Other providers are price-gouging stranded travelers. Noorha offers **fixed, fair pricing** — no surge, no hidden fees.

- People whose Bahrain-bound flights were rerouted to DMM/RUH
- Business travelers arriving at DMM/RUH who need to reach Bahrain
- Families being reunited

### Tertiary: Embassy Staff & Diplomatic Travelers
- Embassy personnel and their families
