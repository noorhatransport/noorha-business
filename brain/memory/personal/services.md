# Services & Offerings

> What you offer, how you package it, and how you deliver value to clients.

---

## What Goes Here

Services define **what you sell and how you deliver it** - the specific offerings, packages, and ways clients can work with you. This helps AI understand your business model and recommend appropriate services in conversations.

### Include
- ✅ Core service offerings with clear descriptions
- ✅ Service packages and tiers
- ✅ Pricing structure and positioning
- ✅ Delivery methods and formats
- ✅ What's included and what's not
- ✅ Ideal client fit for each service

### Don't Include
- ❌ Services you tried once and discontinued
- ❌ Free offerings (those go in content strategy)
- ❌ Vague "consulting" without specifics
- ❌ Services you offer reluctantly

---

## Service Types

### Core Services
**Purpose**: Your primary revenue-generating offerings
**Example**: "6-Week Brand Positioning Sprint" / "Monthly Content Retainer"

### Premium Services
**Purpose**: High-ticket, high-touch offerings
**Example**: "Executive Advisory" / "Done-For-You Campaign"

### Entry Services
**Purpose**: Lower-barrier ways to start working together
**Example**: "Strategy Session" / "Content Audit"

### Productized Services
**Purpose**: Standardized, repeatable offerings
**Example**: "LinkedIn Profile Optimization" / "One-Pager Package"
