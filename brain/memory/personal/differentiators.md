# Differentiators

> What makes you uniquely different and why clients choose you over alternatives.

---

## What Goes Here

Differentiators are **your competitive moats** - the specific attributes, capabilities, approaches, and advantages that make you distinctly different from alternatives. These are the reasons clients choose you instead of competitors, agencies, or DIY solutions.

### Include
- ✅ Specific unique capabilities or methodologies
- ✅ Tangible advantages you deliver
- ✅ Hard-to-replicate assets or expertise
- ✅ Unique combinations of skills/experience
- ✅ Proprietary frameworks or processes
- ✅ Measurable differentiating outcomes

### Don't Include
- ❌ Generic claims anyone could make
- ❌ "We care about quality" type statements
- ❌ Aspirational differentiators you haven't demonstrated
- ❌ Features that are actually table stakes

---

## Differentiator Types

### Capability Differentiators
**Purpose**: Unique skills or abilities you possess
**Example**: "Technical founder who can also write compelling copy"

### Methodology Differentiators
**Purpose**: Proprietary processes or frameworks
**Example**: "Position-First Content Framework"

### Experience Differentiators
**Purpose**: Unique background or expertise combination
**Example**: "10 years as engineer + 5 years content strategist"

### Results Differentiators
**Purpose**: Measurable outcomes you consistently deliver
**Example**: "Avg 3x engagement increase in 90 days"
