## Primary Services

### Cross-Border Transportation (KSA-Bahrain)

**Category**: Premium Taxi Service

**One-Line Description**:
Reliable transportation between Dammam/Khobar and Bahrain via King Fahd Causeway.

**Value Proposition**:
Eliminates the hassle and uncertainty of cross-border travel for business travelers and expats who need dependable, comfortable transport between Saudi Arabia and Bahrain.

**Key Features**:
- **Door-to-door service**: Hotel/office pickup and drop-off on both sides of the causeway
- **Border facilitation**: Experienced drivers familiar with customs procedures
- **Premium comfort**: GMC Yukon Denali, Toyota Sequoia, and Toyota Prado fleet
- **Systematic communication**: WhatsApp coordination and GPS tracking

**Pricing Model**:
Per-trip pricing with advance booking via WhatsApp or phone

**Target Customer**:
International business travelers, expats, and corporate clients needing regular Bahrain-KSA corridor service
