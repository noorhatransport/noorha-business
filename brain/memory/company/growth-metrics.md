## Conversion Funnel

### Ideal Path
```
Search → Website/GMB → Lead Form/Call → Booking

Example:
10,000 searches
→ 1,000 impressions (10% CTR)
→ 100 website visitors (10% click)
→ 5 form submissions (5% conversion)
→ 2 bookings (40% close rate)
```

### February Baseline Funnel
```
? searches
→ 500-1,000 visitors
→ 20-30 leads (2-3% conversion)
→ 10-15 bookings (50% close rate)
```

### May Target Funnel
```
? searches
→ 4,000+ visitors
→ 100+ leads (2.5% conversion)
→ 40+ bookings (40% close rate)
```

**Optimization Focus**:
- Increase traffic (SEO, content, GMB)
- Improve conversion rate (better CTAs, forms, trust signals)
- Maintain close rate (quality leads, professional follow-up)

## Tracking Methodology

### Daily (Automated)
- Google Analytics: Traffic, sources, behavior
- GMB Insights: Views, actions, calls
- Lead dashboard: Submissions and inquiries

### Weekly (Manual)
- Count bookings for the week
- Attribute to source (ask customer)
- Review leading indicators (traffic trend, lead trend)
- Report in standup

### Monthly (Strategic)
- Compare actual vs target bookings
- Analyze funnel (where are we losing/winning?)
- Identify top traffic sources and optimize
- Adjust strategy if needed

## Risk Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Slow Start (Feb bookings flat at 5-10)
**Response**:
- Normal - February is foundation build
- Check leading indicators (traffic growing? leads growing?)
- If indicators growing, bookings will follow in March
- If indicators flat, adjust strategy immediately

### Scenario 2: Mid-Course Miss (Mar bookings only 15 instead of 25)
**Response**:
- Analyze bottleneck (traffic? conversion? close rate?)
- Double down on what's working
- Consider paid ads to supplement organic
- Extend timeline if needed (May 15 vs May 1)

### Scenario 3: Early Success (Mar already at 30+ bookings)
**Response**:
- Amazing! Target achieved early
- Shift focus to sustainability and quality
- Begin partnership discussion earlier
- Plan Saudi office setup ahead of schedule
