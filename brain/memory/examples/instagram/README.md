# Instagram Examples

## What to Include Here

### Quality Criteria
Add Instagram content that demonstrates:
- **Visual hooks**: First 3 seconds that stop the scroll
- **Punchy delivery**: Every word counts, ultra-concise
- **Engaging rhythm**: Visual and verbal pacing
- **Clear value**: One strong insight per piece
- **Strong performance**: High saves, shares, or engagement

### Ideal Count
**5-8 varied examples** covering:
- Different reel script styles
- Various visual approaches
- Different topics/insights
- Range of lengths (15-30 second sweet spot)

## What the AI Will Learn

From your Instagram examples, the AI learns:

### Voice & Tone
- How you communicate in ultra-short format
- Your punchy, engaging style
- How you maintain authenticity in bite-sized content
- Balance of casual and valuable

### Structure
- Hook patterns (visual and verbal)
- How you build to a point in 15-30 seconds
- Pacing and rhythm of delivery
- How you create "save-worthy" moments

### Visual Storytelling
- How you describe desired visual elements
- What visual metaphors you use
- How text and visuals work together
- Your aesthetic preferences

### Engagement
- How you craft thought-provoking questions
- When you use direct address vs. observation
- How you create shareable moments
- What CTAs work for your audience

## Content Types

### Reel Scripts
- **Purpose**: Audio/video content with on-screen text
- **Include**: Full script with timing, visual descriptions
- **Best for**: Teaching AI your verbal delivery style

### Carousels
- **Purpose**: Swipeable educational content
- **Include**: Each slide's text/visual description
- **Best for**: Teaching AI how you break down concepts visually

### Static Posts
- **Purpose**: Single image with caption
- **Include**: Caption copy and visual description
- **Best for**: Teaching AI your caption style
